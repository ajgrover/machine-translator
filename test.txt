That ability to block out the world around you and relax.

The light is paired with a computer program that generates the sequence of lights and links it to music.

Además, los inventores advierten contra el uso por personas que sufren de psicosis.
Furthermore, the inventors warn against use by people suffering from psychosis.

Otros, por su parte, describieron sentirse fuera de sus cuerpos y experimentar una profunda relajación.
Others, for their part, described feeling out of their bodies and experience deep relaxation.

Me sentí ingrávida, no me podía acordar si estaba sentada o de pie porque no podía sentir mi cuerpo.
I felt weightless, I could not decide whether I was sitting or standing because I could not feel my body.
